Ooey Goey Brownies

Description

These brownies are delicious! Through trial-and error we have found the best recipie on brownies. This is an easy guide to make them.

Ingredients

  • Brownie Mix
  • 3 eggs
  • 1 cup of flower
  • bowl
  • wisk
  1. Turn oven on to 365
  2. Mix brownie mix into egggs
  3. Add flower
  4. Wisk until complely mixed
  5. Put in the oven for 25 minutes
  6. Enjoy!
