Cookies Recipe

Description

These cookies are some of the best cookies that money can buy! I don't know what else to write about them but they sure are great!

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Eggs
  • Chocolate Chips
  • Water

Instructions

  1. Heat oven to 325
  2. Mix eggs and flower
  3. Add in chocolate chips
  4. Cook for 15 minutes
  5. Enjoy
